# Glossary

## AI-Native Builder

A developer whose workflow increasingly depends on collaborating effectively with
AI tools such as Cursor.

## Progression Stage

A growth stage in the user's journey, such as `Explorer`, `Growing Builder`, or
`Power User`.

## Mastery Lane

A specific dimension of growth that can be tracked independently from a single
global level, such as workflow quality or project leverage.

## Momentum

The user's ongoing rhythm and consistency with Cursor over time.

## Recovery Loop

A design pattern that helps users resume progress after disruption or inconsistency.

## Insight Mechanic

A feature that reveals meaningful patterns the user would struggle to infer from
memory or raw data alone.

## Product Memory

The durable documentation layer that preserves product context, decisions, and
current state across sessions.

## Clean-Premium Tone

A product voice and visual direction that feels polished, credible, and serious
without becoming sterile.

## Deep Gamification

Gamification rooted in meaningful motivation, mastery, and feedback rather than
surface-level points and badges alone.

## Self-Comparison

Comparing a user against their own past behavior or best performance rather than
against other users.
