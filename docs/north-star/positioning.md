# Positioning

## Product Category

`Momentum` is a personal progression system for the AI-native builder.

It is not only:

- a stats page
- a productivity dashboard
- a gamified skin over usage telemetry

It is the place where a developer can see how their behavior with Cursor is
changing over time and how that behavior connects to mastery, momentum, and real
outcomes.

## Primary Audience

The primary audience is developers actively leveling up with AI tools such as
Cursor. The design starts with the `Growing Builder` stage and helps that user
graduate into `Power User` and later `Strategic Builder` behavior.

## Secondary Contexts

The product should also work for:

- ambitious founders and solo builders shipping products
- busy professionals who want fast, trustworthy signal
- experienced power users who care about leverage and optimization

## Emotional Positioning

The dashboard should feel like:

- a personal command center for becoming an elite AI-native builder
- a source of confidence and clarity
- a premium tool with restrained but real delight

## Tone

Use a hybrid tone with a strong clean-premium bias:

- serious on the outside
- motivating and gameful underneath
- never juvenile
- never corporately sterile

## Differentiation

The product's advantage is that it can reveal patterns that developers could not
easily know otherwise across:

- sessions
- tools
- models
- projects
- streaks
- recovery cycles

That insight layer should make the gamification meaningful instead of decorative.
